ItsJerryAndHarry

ItsJerryAndHarry is a medimum sized Minecraft network with a flourishing (yet occasionally toxic) community. Unlike other server owners the owners (ItsJerry and ItsHarry) remain active on the forums but not so much on the server. Built on the backbone on their highly successful YouTube channels they have hit that "sweet spot" with fun, original minigames that attract a more mature audience. It is the website where I spend a majority of my time. I have been on the J&H network for about two years and I am still extremely active there. I am currently the Helper rank on this server.

SpigotMC

SpigotMC is a website mainly focused around the "Spigot" Minecraft server software. It was created in 2012 as a high-performance fork of CraftBukkit (Formerly the most popular Minecraft server software). Eventually CraftBukkit as a project was shut down but Spigot decided to take the reigns and update both CraftBukkit and Spigot. The amount I am active on here is only seconded to ItsJerryAndHarry. I remain on here to fix bugs and download plugins for Ultra Arcade.
